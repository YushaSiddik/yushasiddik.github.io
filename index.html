About Me

Hi, I'm Abu Bakar Siddik, a Biomedical Engineer and Data Scientist. I specialize in data analysis, machine learning, healthcare solutions, and bioinformatics. My work connects healthcare, research, and technology. I am motivated by the challenge of turning complex data into meaningful insights.

I enjoy solving complex problems, collaborating on interdisciplinary projects, and continuously expanding my skill set in programming and research. I have professional experience in equipment management, system installation, compliance evaluation, and field research. I bring both hands-on technical expertise and analytical problem-solving to my work. My projects often involve collaboration across disciplines such as medical imaging, instrumentation, and computational modeling.
