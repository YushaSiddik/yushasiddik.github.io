About Me

Hi, I'm Abu Bakar Siddik, a Biomedical Engineer and Data Scientist. With a strong passion for technology and innovation, I specialize in data analysis, machine learning, healthcare solutions and bioinformatics. My work connects healthcare, research, and technology. I focus on applying advanced analytical methods to biomedical applications. I am motivated by the challenge of turning complex data into meaningful insights. These insights can improve diagnostics, medical research, and equipment performance.

I enjoy solving complex problems, collaborating on interdisciplinary projects, and continuously expanding my skill set in programming and research. I have professional experience in equipment management, system installation, compliance evaluation, and field research. I bring both hands-on technical expertise and analytical problem-solving to my work. My projects often involve collaboration across disciplines. This includes medical imaging, instrumentation, and computational modeling. I work to ensure that scientific innovation is both reliable and impactful.

My research interests include statistical methods and machine learning techniques. I apply them to uncover patterns in biological data. I also use them to advance biomedical imaging and support evidence-based decision-making. Beyond technical skills, I remain adaptive and solution-oriented. I aim to contribute to the growing field of data-driven healthcare and biomedical innovation. Let's connect and explore how we can make a positive impact together!
